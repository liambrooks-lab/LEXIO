User Manual

1. Choose A Workspace View

Use Dashboard for AI output, Reader for document playback, and AI Studio for glossary and structure review.

2. Import Or Paste Content

Open Reader, upload a supported file or paste content into the source box. LEXIO will rebuild it into a synchronized reading surface.

3. Tune The Voice

Select language, accent, and persona. The exact available voices depend on your system voice pack.

4. Let AI Assist

Use Executive Brief, Study Notes, Presentation Flow, and Q&A prompts from Dashboard to switch your working mode quickly.

5. Read Naturally

Use Start, Pause, Resume, and Stop in Reader. Highlights will follow the spoken text and keep the document centered if focus tracking is enabled.
